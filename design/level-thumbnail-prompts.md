# Level Thumbnail Prompts

## Shared Direction

Use case: stylized concept art. Asset type: 1024 x 1024 square game level-select thumbnail. Create a high-energy 1990s arcade racing-game selection illustration rendered as a premium, colorful low-poly 3D scene. Use a dramatic low camera, a strong central route, bold saturated cinematic lighting, crisp geometry, and one unmistakable landmark. Keep the composition exciting and readable at small size. Do not include text, letters, numbers, logos, UI, watermarks, borders, characters, or photorealism.

Race forward down the center line toward a large funbox and quarter pipe with the iconic shack visible in the upper background. Add restrained motion streaks.
